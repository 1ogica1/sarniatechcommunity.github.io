Join the Community

The best place to see upcoming events is by joining our Facebook Group. If you would like to join the conversation then check out our Slack Group. To get a slack invite and join our mailing list sign up to become a Sarnia Tech Community Member. If you would only like to receive emails about major events then become a Subscriber. We also have a Google Drive where you can check out some of our collaboration docs. PS We take privacy very seriously. We will only use the information you provide to give you information about the Sarnia Tech Community. PPS You can unsubscribe or change your email preferences at any time.
